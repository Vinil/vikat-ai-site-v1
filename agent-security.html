How It Works

How Vikat secures
AI agents.

01 — Deep Discovery

Deep Discovery &
Contextual Insights

Automatically discover every agent within your environment while gaining deep contextual understanding of their complete profile including toolsets, functionality, data access permissions, MCP server connections, and operational behavior.

  • Sanctioned and shadow agent inventory
  • Toolset, knowledge source, and channel mapping
  • Identity and credential discovery
  • Agent-to-agent connection tracing
02 — Blast Radius Analysis

Visualize the
Blast Radius

Map each agent's connections, tools, identities, and knowledge sources to uncover cascading risk scenarios. Intercept dangerous combinations before they reach production.

  • Cascading risk scenario modeling
  • Overprivileged access detection
  • Agent-to-agent lateral movement paths
  • Data exfiltration exposure mapping
03 — Risk Management

Proactive AI Agent
Risk Management

Monitor and detect overprivileged and potentially destructive agent capabilities, and enforce enterprise policies to prevent unauthorized actions and data exposure before they cascade into incidents.

  • Behavioral drift detection and alerting
  • Zero-trust identity enforcement per agent
  • Automated policy violation containment
  • Shadow agent quarantine and governance
04 — Runtime Protection

AI Agent
Runtime Protection

Enforce real-time guardrails on models and agents in production. Detect and block malicious prompts, rogue outputs, and unauthorized agent actions ensuring your AI remains secure, aligned, and compliant.

  • Prompt injection and jailbreak detection
  • Output validation and data leakage prevention
  • Real-time action-level governance
  • Graduated enforcement: observe → warn → block

Enterprise ready for AI innovation at scale.

Excessive Autonomy

Unrestricted decision making combined with access to sensitive data and systems — contained through behavioral boundaries and action-level governance.

Over Permissive Access

Agents accumulate credentials, turning a single compromise into lateral movement — governed through zero-trust identity enforcement.

Excessive Functionality

Expanded capabilities can be abused through malicious inputs or compromised components — controlled through least privilege and tool-level restrictions.

Blast Radius

Compromised agents trigger chain reactions across systems — mitigated through containment and isolation policies.
